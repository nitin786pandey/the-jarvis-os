# Area: Health & Fitness

**Standard to maintain:** Consistent exercise, clean nutrition, energy and vitality
**Active projects:** Weight Loss 15KG

---

## What "good" looks like
- Exercise 5–6 days/week
- Calorie-aware eating (not obsessive, but intentional)
- 3L water daily
- 7+ hours sleep
- 10K steps daily
- Energy levels high, no afternoon crashes

## Recurring habits
| Habit | Frequency | Time |
|-------|-----------|------|
| Workout | 5–6x/week | Morning |
| Calorie tracking | Daily | After each meal |
| Water intake | Daily | Throughout day |
| Step count check | Daily | Evening |
| Sleep by 11 PM | Daily | Night |
| Meal prep | 1x/week | Sunday |
| Weigh-in | 1x/week | Sunday morning |

## Metrics I track
- Weight (weekly)
- Workout completion rate (weekly)
- Average daily steps (weekly)
- Energy level (subjective 1–10, daily)
- Sleep hours (daily)

## When this area generates a new project
- If weight loss stalls for 3+ weeks → reassess approach
- If injury occurs → recovery project
- When 15KG goal is hit → shift to maintenance/muscle building project
