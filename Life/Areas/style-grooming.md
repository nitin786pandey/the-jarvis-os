# Area: Style & Grooming

**Standard to maintain:** Intentional appearance, healthy skin, put-together presentation
**Active projects:** Skin Health Transformation, Fashion Upgrade

---

## What "good" looks like
- Skincare routine is automatic (AM and PM, no skips)
- Dressed intentionally every day — even at home
- Grooming is consistent (hair, beard, nails)
- Clothes fit well and express personal style
- Getting occasional compliments on appearance

## Recurring habits
| Habit | Frequency | Time |
|-------|-----------|------|
| AM skincare routine | Daily | Morning (10 min) |
| PM skincare routine | Daily | Evening (10 min) |
| Intentional outfit selection | Daily | Morning (2–5 min) |
| Grooming check | Daily | Morning |
| Style inspiration browsing | 1x/week | 30 min |
| Wardrobe review | 1x/month | 30 min |
| Exfoliation | 2x/week | PM routine |
| Face mask | 1x/week | Weekend PM |

## Monthly budget allocation
| Category | Monthly Budget |
|----------|---------------|
| Skincare products | As needed (replenish) |
| Clothing | Planned purchases only |
| Grooming (haircut, etc.) | 1x/month |

## Quarterly wardrobe review questions
1. What 5 items did I wear most?
2. What never got worn? (Donate/archive)
3. What 1–2 pieces would upgrade my wardrobe most?
4. Am I developing a consistent style?
