# Area: Photography

**Standard to maintain:** Regular practice, improving technique, building a visual eye
**Active projects:** Concert Photography

---

## What "good" looks like
- Shooting regularly (not just when inspired)
- Understanding exposure triangle intuitively
- Developing a consistent editing style
- Building a portfolio that shows progression
- Engaging with the photography community

## Recurring habits
| Habit | Frequency | Time |
|-------|-----------|------|
| Photography study/theory | 3x/week (weekdays) | 30 min evening |
| Photo editing practice | 2x/week | 30–45 min |
| Photo walk / outing | 1x/week (weekend) | 1–2 hours |
| Analyze other photographers' work | 1x/week | 15 min |
| Post to portfolio/Instagram | 1x/week | — |

## Skills progression
| Skill | Current Level | Target by Dec 2026 |

| Manual exposure | Beginner | Intuitive |

1. Concert / live music (primary goal)
