## Weekly Exercise Plan

| Day | Type | Duration | Focus |
|-----|------|----------|-------|
| Mon | Strength | 45–60 min | Upper body |
| Tue | Cardio | 30–45 min | Running / cycling |
| Wed | Strength | 45–60 min | Lower body |
| Thu | Cardio | 30–45 min | HIIT / swimming |
| Fri | Strength | 45–60 min | Full body |
| Sat | Active outdoor | 60–90 min | Hiking / sports / long walk |
| Sun | Active recovery | 30 min | Yoga / stretching / walk |

## Daily Non-Negotiables
- Track all meals (calorie counting app)
- Drink 3L water minimum
- 10,000 steps
- Sleep 7+ hours
- No sugar drinks, minimize processed food
