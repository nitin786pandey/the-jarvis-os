# Project: Skin Health Transformation

**Connected area:** Style & Grooming
**Status:** Active
**Target:** Clear, healthy, glowing skin
**Timeline:** Visible improvement by June 2026 (4 months)
**Approach:** Consistent routine + hydration + diet + professional guidance

---

## Daily Routines

### AM Routine (~10 min)
1. Gentle cleanser (CeraVe / Cetaphil type)
2. Toner (optional — hydrating, not stripping)
3. Vitamin C serum (antioxidant, brightening)
4. Moisturizer (lightweight, non-comedogenic)
5. Sunscreen SPF 50 (NON-NEGOTIABLE, even indoors)

### PM Routine (~10 min)
1. Double cleanse (oil cleanser → water cleanser)
2. Toner
3. Treatment serum (Niacinamide for pores/texture OR Retinol for anti-aging)
4. Moisturizer (richer than AM)
5. Lip balm / eye cream (optional)

### Weekly Treatments
- **Exfoliation:** 2x/week (chemical exfoliant — AHA/BHA)
- **Face mask:** 1x/week (hydrating or clay, depending on need)
- **Beard care:** If applicable — oil, balm, clean trim

## Lifestyle Factors (These Matter More Than Products)
- **Water:** 3L minimum daily
- **Sleep:** 7+ hours (skin repairs during sleep)
- **Diet:** Reduce sugar, dairy (if acne-prone), processed food
- **Stress:** Manage through exercise, meditation, boundaries
- **Pillowcase:** Change every 3–4 days (silk recommended)
- **Hands off face:** Stop touching your face

## Milestones

- [ ] Week 1–2: Establish AM/PM routine consistently
- [ ] Month 1: Routine is automatic, skin adjusting
- [ ] Month 2: Visible texture improvement
- [ ] Month 3: Reduced breakouts/dark spots
- [ ] Month 4: Compliments start coming in
